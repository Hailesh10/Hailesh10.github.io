Research Focus

Flood Early Warning System

citizen science · satellite RS · ML

Fusing crowd-sourced gauges, MODIS/Sentinel-1 and 2, and HEC-HMS to deliver actionable forecasts for threshold based flood early warning in data-scarce environments. Adopted by Ethiopian disaster agency.

ongoing

Coupling groundwater flow and Heat Transport

coupled groundwater–surface water

Fully coupled numerical model of groundwater‑heat transport in sub‑arctic riverbanks – quantifying freeze‑thaw dynamics & lateral erosion (DIWA Flagship).
