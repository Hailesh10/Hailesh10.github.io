Welcome

I am a Postdoctoral Researcher at Aalto University working on hydrology, hydrogeology, groundwater–surface water interactions, climate resilience, numerical modelling, remote sensing, and machine learning for water resources.

My research combines physically-based models, artificial intelligence, and citizen science to improve flood forecasting, groundwater modelling, and climate adaptation for vulnerable regions.
