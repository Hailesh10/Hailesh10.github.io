Postdoctoral Researcher · Aalto University

Water security under a changing climate

Senior hydrologist and hydrogeologist with 10+ years across academia, international development and consulting. My work spans flood early warning systems that fuse citizen science, satellite remote sensing and machine learning; coupled groundwater–surface water modelling including heat transport in sub-arctic riverbanks; and the translation of hydrological science into climate-resilient water policy across Africa, Europe and the Middle East.

01 · Forecast

Flood early warning

Citizen-science and ML-augmented forecasting for data-scarce catchments, from Ethiopian highlands to peri-urban basins.

02 · Subsurface

Groundwater & heat transport

Fully coupled groundwater–surface water models resolving freeze–thaw dynamics and lateral erosion in sub-arctic riverbanks.

03 · Policy

Water risk governance

Bridging hydrological science and national disaster-risk and infrastructure policy across three continents.
